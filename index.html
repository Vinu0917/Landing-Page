The Problem

Traditional Hela Bojunhala operations rely heavily on manual processes, resulting in slow service, poor coordination, lack of transparency, and limited digital support.

The Solution

A unified digital platform that connects customers, cashiers, drivers, vendors, and administrators to automate workflow and optimize operations.

System Overview

Our system consists of four interconnected portals designed to cover the complete workflow of Hela Bojunhala services.

Customer Portal

Users can browse menus, place online orders, make secure payments, and track their deliveries in real-time.

Cashier Portal

Cashiers manage order confirmations, monitor kitchen status, and coordinate with drivers seamlessly.

Driver Portal

Drivers receive delivery tasks, update progress, and complete orders efficiently with real-time instructions.

Admin Portal

Admins oversee all operations, manage vendors, review reports, and analyze business insights.

System Impact

Streamlined Operations

Reduces manual tasks and automates essential workflows.

Faster Service

Improves delivery time and order processing efficiency.

Better Decision Making

Provides reports and analytics to support strategic decisions.

Enhanced User Experience

Smooth and efficient experience for all user types.
